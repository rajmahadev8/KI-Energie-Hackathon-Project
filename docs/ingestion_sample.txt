Simplified rules for balcony power plants (plug-in solar devices) since Solarpaket I (2024)

Plug-in solar devices ("balcony power plants") have been significantly simplified. Registration in the
Marktstammdatenregister has been reduced to a few details; a separate registration with the grid operator
is no longer required. Modules of up to 2000 Watt of installed capacity are permitted, and the
inverter may feed up to 800 Voltampere (VA) into the home grid. As a transitional measure, even
backward-running meters are tolerated until the metering point operator swaps in a suitable meter.
A Schuko plug is tolerated by the standards. Tenants and homeowners have a fundamental right to
consent for the installation.
